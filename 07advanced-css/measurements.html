Measurments

Here at Baking Basics we will be using the U.S. measurements for our recipes. If you are in need of converting a measurement, try out this conversion chart:

All Recipes

U.S. Baking Measurments

Measuring Spoons

  • 1 tablespoon (Tbsp) = 3 teaspoons (tsp)
  • 1 teaspoon (tsp)
  • 1/2 tsp
  • 1/4 tsp

Dry Measuring Cups

  • 1 cup
  • 3/4 cup
  • 1/2 cup
  • 1/3 cup
  • 1/4 cup

Liquid Measuring Cups

  • 8 oz - 16 oz glass or plastic

Temperature

  • Oven temperatures are in FAHRENHEIT
  • Common fahrenheit temperatures include: 325, 350, 275, and 400.

Quick Conversion Tips

  • 1 cup = 16 Tbsp
  • 1 Tbsp = 3 Tsp
  • 1 cup (liquid) = 8 fluid oz.
  • 1 pint = 2 cups
  • 1 quart = 4 cups
  • 1 gallon = 16 cups
