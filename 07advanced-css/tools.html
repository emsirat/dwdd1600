Baking Tools

Baking equipment is essential for accuracy, texture, and most importantly, making the bakers life easier.

Measuring and Mixing

Dry Measuring Cups
For ingredients like flour and sugar.
Wet Measuring Cups
For ingredients like milk, water, and oil.
Measuring spoons
For smaller amounts like baking powder, salt, and vanilla.
Mixing Bowls
A set of 2–3 bowls in different sizes is great. Choose ones that are sturdy and easy to clean (glass or stainless steel are popular)
Whisk
For mixing dry ingredients or combining wet ingredients smoothly.
